Welcome to Anglo's Math Club!

We’re excited to introduce our newest initiative: Conquer The Numbers! This program is designed for students who enjoy problem-solving, logical thinking, and challenging themselves with math beyond the classroom. Through fun activities, friendly competitions, and team challenges, members will strengthen their skills in algebra, geometry, and strategic thinking. If you like working with numbers and want to push your abilities further in a supportive and engaging environment, this is the place for you. Are you ready to conquer the numbers?

Conquer the Numbers

Conquer The Number is our latest challenge. In it students are faced with weekly problems that they can answer to earn points. They can see their progress in a live leaderboard, and earn points not only for themselves, but for their houses too!
